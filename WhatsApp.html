What is WhatsApp Business

Professional communication platform for businesses of all sizes

Overview

WhatsApp Business is the official business version of WhatsApp designed specifically for business communication. It offers advanced features like business profiles, product catalogs, automated messaging, and analytics.



Key Differences

  • Business profile instead of personal
  • Product catalog feature
  • Analytics and insights
  • Multiple device support

Why Use It?

  • Professional appearance
  • Better customer trust
  • Advanced features
  • Free to use

How to Install & Set Up WhatsApp Business

Step-by-step guide to get started with WhatsApp Business

1

Download WhatsApp Business

Download the app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store. It's free for all users.

2

Install & Open App

Install the app on your device and open it to begin the setup process.

3

Verify Phone Number

Verify your business phone number with a code received via SMS. This confirms you own the number.

4

Create Business Profile

Add your business name, profile photo, category, and other business details.

5

Set Up Business Info

Configure business hours, address, email, website, and description for customers.

6

Start Using Features

Begin using catalogs, quick replies, automated messages, and other business tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about WhatsApp Business and integration

Is WhatsApp Business really free?

Yes, WhatsApp Business is completely free to use for all businesses. There are no hidden charges or subscription fees for the basic features.

Can I use both WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business?

Yes, you can use both apps on the same device. However, for business use, we recommend using WhatsApp Business exclusively for better organization.

How many products can I add to the catalog?

You can add up to 500 products to your WhatsApp Business catalog. Each product can have multiple images, descriptions, and pricing options.

What is N8N and how does it work with WhatsApp?

N8N is an automation tool that connects with WhatsApp Business API. It can automate responses, manage leads, integrate with other systems, and create complex workflows - all through WhatsApp.

Can I accept payments through WhatsApp?

Yes! WhatsApp Business supports in-app payments through third-party payment providers. We can help you set up secure payment collection for your products and services.
