Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about WhatsApp integration

How does WhatsApp integration work?

We integrate WhatsApp by adding click-to-chat buttons, floating widgets, and contact forms that directly open WhatsApp with pre-filled messages. Users can contact you with a single click, and messages go directly to your WhatsApp number.

Is it mobile friendly?

Yes, absolutely! WhatsApp integration is perfect for mobile users as it opens the native WhatsApp app on their device. This provides a seamless experience that customers are already familiar with.

Can messages be prefilled?

Yes! We can pre-fill messages based on the context. For example, product pages can have messages like "I'm interested in [Product Name]" and service pages can have "I'd like to know more about [Service Name]".

Where can buttons be placed?

WhatsApp buttons can be placed anywhere on your website - header, footer, floating widget, product pages, service pages, contact sections, and even within forms. We'll strategically place them based on your business needs.

Do I need a WhatsApp Business account?

While not mandatory, we recommend using WhatsApp Business for better features like automated responses, business profiles, and analytics. However, regular WhatsApp works perfectly for basic integration.
