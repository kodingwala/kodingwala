Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Can I add custom sections to my portfolio?

Yes, we can add custom sections based on your specific needs. Whether you need a blog, testimonials, case studies, or any other section, we can customize your portfolio accordingly.

Can you link my resume to the portfolio?

Absolutely! We can integrate your resume into the portfolio with a download button, embed it as a section, or create a dedicated page for it. We'll discuss the best approach based on your needs.

How long does it take to deliver a portfolio website?

Typically, we deliver portfolio websites within 5-7 working days, depending on the complexity and number of projects to be included. We'll provide a specific timeline after understanding your requirements.

Do you provide hosting for the portfolio?

We don't provide hosting directly, but we can guide you in choosing the right hosting provider based on your needs and budget. We'll also help you deploy your portfolio to the hosting server.

Do you help with domain registration?

Yes, we can help you choose and register a domain name for your portfolio. We'll suggest appropriate domain names and guide you through the registration process.
