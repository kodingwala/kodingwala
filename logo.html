Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our logo design services?

How many revisions do I get?

You get up to 3 revisions on your selected logo concept. Additional revisions can be discussed based on your requirements. We want to ensure you're completely satisfied with your final logo.

What is the delivery time?

Standard logo designs are delivered within 3-5 business days. Complex mascot logos and brand identity packages may take 7-10 days. We'll provide a timeline with our quote.

What file types do I receive?

You receive high-resolution PNG, JPG, and vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) for maximum flexibility. We also provide files optimized for web, print, and social media use.

Can you create a custom branding style?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom branding styles that match your vision. Share your preferences, industry, and target audience, and we'll create something unique for you.

Do you create custom logos?

Yes, all our logos are custom-designed based on your specific requirements. We don't use templates - each logo is uniquely created for your brand.
