Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

How long does it take to deliver the presentation?

Delivery time depends on the complexity and number of slides. Simple presentations with 10-15 slides can be delivered within 2-3 days, while more complex presentations may take 5-7 days. We'll provide a specific timeline after understanding your requirements.

How many slides are included in the package?

Our standard package includes up to 15 slides. Additional slides can be added at an extra cost. We'll discuss your specific slide requirements during the consultation.

Can you customize the theme according to my needs?

Yes, we create custom themes based on your brand colors, logo, and specific requirements. We'll work closely with you to ensure the presentation aligns with your vision and needs.

Will I get an editable file of the presentation?

Yes, we deliver the final presentation in both PPT and PDF formats. The PPT file is fully editable, allowing you to make minor changes in the future if needed.

How many revisions are included in the package?

Our standard package includes up to 2 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final design. Additional revisions can be accommodated at a nominal fee.
