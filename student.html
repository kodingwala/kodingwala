Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time varies depending on the complexity of the project. Simple resumes and PPTs can be delivered within 2-3 days, while mini projects and portfolio websites may take 5-10 days. We'll provide a timeline with our quote.

What types of projects do you support?

We support a wide range of academic projects including web development, UI/UX design, data science projects, and more. Contact us with your specific requirements.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! All our services are already priced with students in mind. We offer special student-friendly pricing and additional discounts for bulk orders or group projects.

Can you do custom work based on my requirements?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom solutions tailored to your specific requirements. Just share your project details, and we'll create a solution that meets your needs.

Do you provide support for portfolio websites?

Yes, we provide complete support for portfolio websites including design, development, hosting guidance, and even content suggestions to make your portfolio stand out.
