Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

What details are needed for the resume design?

We'll need your personal information, educational background, work experience (if any), skills, achievements, and any specific preferences for the design. You can provide this information through our WhatsApp consultation.

How long does it take to deliver the resume?

We typically deliver the first draft within 2-3 working days after receiving all necessary information. After your feedback, we make revisions and deliver the final version within 1-2 days.

Will I get an editable format of the resume?

Yes, we provide both the final PDF version for immediate use and an editable source file (MS Word or Google Docs) so you can make minor updates in the future.

Do you provide PDF support for the resume?

Absolutely! We deliver the final resume in high-quality PDF format that maintains perfect formatting across all devices and is ready for both digital submission and printing.

How many revisions are included in the package?

Our standard package includes up to 3 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final design. Additional revisions can be accommodated at a nominal fee.
