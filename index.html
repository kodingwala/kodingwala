Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Do you create mobile responsive websites?

Yes, all our websites are fully responsive and work perfectly on all devices including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones. We use a mobile-first approach to ensure the best user experience across all screen sizes.

Is domain included in static website price?

No, the domain cost is separate from our website design package. However, we can help you purchase and set up your domain. The static website price includes the design, development, and WhatsApp integration only.

Do you provide WhatsApp integration?

Yes, WhatsApp integration is included in our static website package. We can add WhatsApp chat buttons, click-to-chat links, and other WhatsApp features to help you connect with your customers easily.

Can I order only a resume or PPT?

Absolutely! You can order individual services like resume design or PPT design without purchasing a website. We offer all our services as standalone packages with affordable pricing.

Can students order mini projects?

Yes, we offer special services for students including mini projects, portfolio websites, resume designs, and more at student-friendly prices. We provide proper documentation and explanations to help you learn.

How is dynamic website pricing decided?

Dynamic website pricing is custom and depends on various factors including the number of pages, complexity of features, database requirements, admin panel functionality, forms, and specific business needs. Contact us with your requirements for a custom quote.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time varies depending on the complexity of the project. Simple static websites and resumes can be delivered within 3-5 days, while more complex dynamic websites may take 2-4 weeks. We'll provide a timeline with our quote.
