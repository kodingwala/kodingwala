Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

How long does it take to deliver a static website?

Typically, we deliver static websites within 5-7 working days, depending on the complexity and number of pages. We'll provide a specific timeline after understanding your requirements.

Do you provide domain registration?

Domain registration is not included in our static website package, but we can guide you through the process of purchasing and setting up your domain. We'll help you choose the right domain name and registrar.

Do you provide hosting services?

We don't provide hosting directly, but we offer guidance on choosing the right hosting provider based on your needs and budget. We'll also help you deploy your website to the hosting server.

How many pages are included in the static website package?

Our standard static website package includes up to 5 pages (Home, About, Services, Gallery, and Contact). Additional pages can be added at an extra cost. We'll discuss your specific page requirements during the consultation.

How does WhatsApp integration work?

We integrate WhatsApp by adding click-to-chat buttons and links throughout your website. When visitors click these buttons, it opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message, making it easy for them to contact you directly.
